## About Bulk SMS

Bulk SMS is an effective communication solution that allows businesses to send thousands of text messages to customers instantly. It is commonly used for promotional campaigns, customer notifications, appointment reminders, OTP verification, and business announcements.

With fast delivery, excellent mobile reach, and high open rates, SMS remains one of the most trusted communication channels for businesses across various industries.
